About the Conference

Two days International Conference on “Ethical Challenges in Recent Technologies in Engineering” is to bring together experts, researchers, and innovators from around the world to address the profound ethical dilemmas posed by cutting-edge technologies on to a common forum. The aim of the conference is to provide a dynamic platform for researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders from diverse backgrounds to Examine Ethical Dilemmas, Promote Ethical Awareness, Facilitate Knowledge Exchange, Foster Collaboration, contribute to Ethical Frameworks, Shape the Future of Engineering Ethics and Advance Ethical Technology

  • The primary goal of this conference is to promote research and development activities in Communication a n d Electrical Technologies This aims to bring together researchers and scientists from academia, industry, and research laboratories to present new results and identify future research directions in innovative techniques and its applications.
  • This naturally facilitates sustained research and exchange of ideas in the various areas.

About DMI

  • DMI Engineering College is a Christian Minority Technical Educational Institution, approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The college is promoted and managed by the Sisters of DMI, which was founded in the year 2009 by Rev.Fr. Dr.J.E.Arul Raj.
  • The society manages several educational, technical, health, welfare and social development institutions.Our mission is to produce creative and technically strong engineers and to research pioneering solutions to global challenges. We do this with an unprecedented commitment to integrating across engineering, sciences, arts, business and other disciplines to yield transformative results.
